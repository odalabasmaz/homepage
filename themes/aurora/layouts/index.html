About

Who I am

I'm a Principal Software Engineer based in Munich, Germany. Over the past 15+ years, I've designed and operated production systems that serve millions of users, built and grown engineering teams, and driven technical and organisational decisions at scale.

I've been at Atlassian since 2017 — first as a Site Reliability Engineer at Opsgenie (pre-acquisition), then as Engineering Manager leading the Change Management team under Jira Service Management. Along the way I've worn the SRE, EM, and IC hats simultaneously, which gives me an unusually wide view of what it takes to ship reliable software fast.

I care deeply about observability, reliability, and the operational reality of systems once they're in production. A good system is one that doesn't wake people up at 3am — and when it does, recovers gracefully.
